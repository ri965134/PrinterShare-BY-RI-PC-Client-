**PrinterShare BY RI** is a lightweight, portable Windows PC client that enables seamless remote printing from your Android device. It acts as a secure bridge, allowing you to wirelessly print documents, PDFs, and images from your mobile phone to any standard USB or network printer connected to your computer—without the need for complex network sharing setups or cables.

## ✨ Key Features

* **Instant Mobile-to-PC Printing:** Intercepts print commands from the PrinterShare Android app and routes them directly to your default Windows printer in seconds.
* **100% Portable (Zero Installation):** Delivered as a single, lightweight executable (`.exe`) file with an embedded print engine. No heavy installations or third-party drivers required.
* **Always Ready (Background Operation):** Runs quietly in the Windows system tray. It features automatic session persistence, meaning it reconnects and stays ready to print even after your PC wakes up from sleep or hibernation mode.
* **Smart Queue Management:** Automatically cleans up printer data upon disconnection, ensuring no "ghost printers" or stuck print queues are left behind on your system.

## 📥 Download & Installation

This software does not require installation. Just download and run!

1. **Download:** Go to the [v1.0.0 Release Page](https://github.com/ri965134/PrinterShare-BY-RI-PC-Client-/releases/tag/v1.0.0) and download the `PrinterShare_BY_RI.exe` file.
2. **Run:** Double-click the downloaded `.exe` file. (If prompted by Windows Defender, click "More info" and "Run anyway").
3. **Connect:** The client will appear in your system tray. Open the **PrinterShare BY RI** app on your Android device to connect to your PC.
4. **Print:** Select your document on your mobile device and hit print!

*Note: Ensure your Windows default printer is set to the physical printer you intend to use.*

## 💻 System Requirements

* **Operating System:** Windows 10 or Windows 11.
* **Network:** Both the PC and the Android device must have an active internet connection.
* **Companion App:** Requires the PrinterShare BY RI Android application download from Play Store.
